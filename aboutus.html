About Us

We are STEM students enrolled at Saint Michael College of Caraga, proudly identifying ourselves as a group known as "SINIGENGGENG." Our group is composed of five dedicated members who share a passion for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). As a whole, we bring together our skills and knowledge in creating a website that mirrors our dedication as students in STEM and as Michaelinians.


The name "SINIGENGGENG" reflects not only our unity but also our unique identity. Each letter represents a crucial aspect of our group, symbolizing our commitment to Support, Inspire, Nurture, Innovate, Grow, Engage, Network, and Generate Excellence in the fields of STEM.


Being STEM enthusiasts, we constantly strive and advocate for academic excellence; hence why our educational website can serve as a guiding light for our fellow students to further expand their knowledge in the field of Earth Science.
